Principles over promises

⚡
Instant & light Plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No build step, no trackers, nothing between you and the app.
🔒
Local-first Everything works without an account. Your data lives in your browser; connecting a backend is always your choice.
🌱
Open source, GPL-3 Every app, and the platform behind them, is free software. Self-host the whole suite, backend included.
✨
Joy is a feature Elegant, playful, mobile-first. Ships almost daily — every app tells you what's new, honestly.
